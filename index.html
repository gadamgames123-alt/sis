SIS / ABOUT THE UNIT
Special Investigation Section
LOS ANGELES POLICE DEPARTMENT — DETECTIVE BUREAU — EST. 1965
The Special Investigation Section (SIS) is an elite plainclothes detective unit within the LAPD Detective Bureau. Operating under strict confidentiality, SIS specializes in long-term covert surveillance of career criminals, gathering evidence for successful prosecution of major crimes including homicide, robbery, narcotics trafficking, and organized crime. SIS detectives maintain extended surveillance on subjects—sometimes for weeks or months—building comprehensive criminal cases. The unit is known for its high-risk operations and has been instrumental in apprehending Los Angeles' most dangerous career criminals.
